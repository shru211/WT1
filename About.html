ABOUT US


In this cafe, vegetarian food is recommended. Paneer and white sauce pasta are available at FRIENDS Bistro. A good chocolate custard, chocolate waffle, or chocolate chip cookie is a treat. After a long day at the office, you can relax with a cup of Irish coffee in hand. Ice tea, cold coffee, and Americano are all great options to consider when ordering.

This place is great because of the hard-working, positive, and friendly staff. Service is praised by visitors as being excellent at this establishment. You’ll get a good deal on food at this cafe. The blissful ambiance and divine decor will certainly appeal to you…

The theme of The Friends Bistro café is based on the Friends Web series / show that will transport you into the characters’ world. So, if you’re a great fan of the Friends show and you’re traveling in Nashik, check out The Friends Bistro – one of the best cafes in Nashik.
